INTRODUCTION

I was born Pamela Jean Plumlee on October 14th, 1958. I am a native Californian. I worked as a Licensed Psychiatric Technician at Lanterman Developmental Center for 23 years. Primarily with adult men with developmental disabilities and behavior issues. I miss my clients and hope they are all flourishing in their new homes. Due to the closure of LDC I am now retired and enjoying it very much! I think I will probably re-enter the work force in the future but I'm not sure what I want to do. Currently I drive an Acura Tsx (it handles corners nicely) and/or a Chevrolet Truck that is a beast. I love animals and currently have two dogs and a cat. My bearded dragon recently died at 15 years old. Over the years as the boys grew up we've had just about every pet from snakes to guinea pigs, even a legless lizard.
